Characteristics

Characteristics of Authoritarian (Dictatorial) style parenting

Pros

Unlike the uninvolved parenting style, this child has a parent that is active in their life. However, the child may not look forward to being around the parent or spending any time with them whatsoever.

Learn more »

Cons

The authoritarian style parent "combines low levels of warmth with high levels of control" and resembles a dictatorial politician. It often includes one-way communication from parent to child.

Learn more »

Effects on Children

Authoritarian style parenting has been associated with 
 "hostility, delinquency, rebelliousness, and antisocial aggression" in children. This style of parenting is also often coupled with child depression and high anxiety.

Learn more »
